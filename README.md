BeYou Studio — Lead Magnet Project

Overview
BeYou Studio is a modern beauty and wellness concept focused on delivering personalized skincare solutions. Built around the idea that “your skin is trying to tell you something,” this project creates an interactive lead magnet that helps users discover their unique skin profile while enabling salons to capture high-quality leads.

Objective
To design and build an interactive lead magnet that:
Engages users through personalization
Solves real skin concerns
Captures user contact details (name & phone/email)
Converts interest into qualified leads for a salon/spa

Concept: Know Your Skin Story
The core idea is to move beyond generic beauty advice and help users understand their “skin story.”

Users take a short quiz and receive:
A personalized skin profile
A summary of their skin condition
A recommended treatment plan

Lead Magnet: Interactive Quiz
Format: 5-question skincare quiz
Focus: Lifestyle, skin behavior, and concerns
Output: Personalized skin persona + treatment plan
Lead Capture: Name + phone/email before results

This creates a value exchange — users receive tailored insights in return for their contact details.

Customer Personas
The quiz maps users into one of four dominant skin profiles:
The Glow Cultivator → Pigmentation, dullness
The Hydration Devotee → Dryness, sensitivity
The Balance Seeker → Oily/combination skin
The Ageless Radiant → Early aging concerns
